Social & Community Engagement: Building a Better Tomorrow

  • **Dedicated Social Worker:** A tireless advocate, organizing and participating in diverse community welfare initiatives.
  • **Health & Wellness Crusader:** Orchestrates vital Blood Donation and Health Check-up Camps for students, seniors, and marginalized communities, with a special focus on empowering downtrodden women.
  • **Architect of Awareness:** Leads impactful Social Awareness Camps, promoting:

  • **Empowerment Through Skill:** Establishes and manages Skill Development Centres, igniting entrepreneurial spirit and providing crucial training (Technical, Non-Technical, Agriculture) to youth and SHGs.
